Scale at Speed

Our startup clients need to keep ahead. With our help, they have transitioned to keep pace with growing customer demand and move into new markets. We loved working with them and want to do the same for you.

NZ digital startups are great at innovation, but often struggle with delivery. And we hate it when a great idea attracts genuine demand but fails in the execution.

We work with clients who recognise this problem. With one client over the course of a year, we achieved:

  • 8X increase in delivery speed to onboard clients
  • 200% increase in developer productivity
  • 50% monthly reduction in hosting costs
  • 270% increase in annual recurring revenue

And through all that growth and change, quality improved. So, what’s the problem that we solved?

Pace of delivery slows

Initial MVPs are necessarily “fast and cheap”. But often, when it comes time to grow, delivery starts to take twice as long and cost twice as much. The slow pace of delivery eventually puts pressure on cashflow, and the tech team becomes an unbearable cost. We call this “scale fail”.

Profitability potential not realised

Ultimately, the potential profitability of the company is not realised—so the business owner loses, investors lose and the NZ economy loses.

Transition to meet demand

Startups need to be able to transition from one “right-scale” to the next as demand grows.

Technical execution partner

We have experience of remediating these issues before companies hit scale fail. We can rapidly help to right-scale the technical execution at the critical stage between product/market-fit validation and scaling.
