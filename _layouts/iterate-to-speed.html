To do this we look for them to:

  • Engage

    As the drive to deliver increases and tech stacks become more complex, collaboration becomes more critical. Teams are at the heart of the way we work and that requires everyone to work together. Let's be willing and ready to listen, ask and respond.

  • Act

    Change can be overwhelming in complex or even chaotic situations. That's when it is even more important to try small changes—to watch and learn from the response. Only then will we know if what we did was right.

  • Focus

    We will need you to make tough trade-off decisions to enable the team to focus on the most valuable work first. This is a dynamic process at the strategy and product roadmap level where you will negotiate short and long-term goals.

  • Learn

    We need to meet the challenge of building out our breadth and depth of technology and product development skills. But being able to adapt and learn is as important as building skills. Let’s explore new ideas and ways of working to find the right fit for your business.
